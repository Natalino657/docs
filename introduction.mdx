ShopFront is a mobile shopping app that lets you discover products, build a cart, and complete a purchase from your phone, tablet, or browser. Whether you shop on an iPhone, an Android device, or directly in a web browser, you get the same streamlined experience: a fast product catalog with search, a persistent cart, and a secure PayPal checkout — all without switching between multiple apps or websites.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Product browsing and search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Search the full catalog from the home screen, filter by category, and page through results. Every product page shows images, descriptions, pricing, and customer reviews.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Shopping cart" icon="cart-shopping">
    Add items from any product page and revisit your cart at any time. Quantities update instantly and your cart persists across sessions so you never lose your selections.
  </Card>
  <Card title="PayPal checkout" icon="credit-card">
    Pay securely with your PayPal account in a few taps. No need to manually enter card details — ShopFront hands off to PayPal and returns you to the app once payment is complete.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Order tracking" icon="truck">
    After you place an order, follow its progress from your profile. Each order shows its current status and a full history of updates from purchase through delivery.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Product reviews" icon="star">
    Read verified reviews from other customers before you buy. After your order arrives, you can leave your own rating and written review directly in the app.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Account management" icon="user">
    Update your name, email, password, and saved addresses from your profile. Your order history and preferences stay in sync across every platform you use.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  PayPal is the sole payment method in ShopFront. You need an active PayPal account to complete a purchase. Browsing the catalog and managing your cart does not require PayPal.
</Note>

## Supported platforms

ShopFront runs natively on iOS and Android and is also accessible in any modern web browser. You do not need to install anything to use the web version — open the link and start shopping.

| Platform | How to access |
|---|---|
| iOS | Download from the Apple App Store |
| Android | Download from Google Play |
| Web | Open in any modern browser via the ShopFront web URL |

## Checkout flow

Every purchase follows the same five-stage flow, designed to get you from browsing to a confirmed order as quickly as possible.

1. **Browse** — Search or scroll the catalog to find what you want.
2. **Cart** — Add one or more products and review quantities and pricing.
3. **Shipping** — Enter or confirm your delivery address.
4. **Payment** — Authenticate with PayPal to authorize the charge.
5. **Order placed** — Receive a confirmation and start tracking your order from your profile.

Each stage is a single screen. You can go back and make changes at any point before you reach the PayPal authorization step.
