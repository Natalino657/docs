Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning customer, this page covers the questions we hear most often. If you cannot find the answer you need here, check the [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) guide or open an issue on GitHub.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need an account to shop on ShopFront?">
    You can browse all products and view their details without creating an account. However, you must be signed in to proceed through checkout. When you tap **Proceed to Checkout** from the cart, you will be prompted to sign in or register if you are not already logged in. Creating an account is free and only takes a moment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods does ShopFront accept?">
    ShopFront currently accepts **PayPal only**. During checkout you will be directed to the PayPal payment flow to complete your purchase. Make sure your PayPal account has sufficient funds or a valid payment method linked before you begin checkout.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track my order?">
    After placing an order, open the **Orders** tab in the app. Each order displays its current status:

    - **Payment pending** — your payment has not been captured yet.
    - **Paid** — your payment was successful and your order is being prepared for delivery.
    - **Delivered** — your order has been dispatched and delivered.

    The paid and delivered timestamps are shown alongside each status so you always know exactly when each milestone occurred.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel or modify an order after placing it?">
    Orders cannot be cancelled or modified once they have been placed. If you need to make a change, please contact our support team as soon as possible and include your order details. We will do our best to assist you, but we cannot guarantee changes can be made after payment has been captured.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why is my PayPal payment not going through?">
    There are a few common reasons a payment may fail:

    - Your PayPal account may have insufficient funds or no valid payment method linked.
    - Your internet connection may have dropped during the payment flow.
    - PayPal may have flagged the transaction for security reasons.

    Check your PayPal account balance and payment methods, ensure you have a stable internet connection, and then try again. If the problem continues, log in to PayPal directly to see whether there are any alerts or restrictions on your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I leave a product review?">
    To submit a review you must be signed in to your ShopFront account. Navigate to the product page for the item you want to review, scroll to the **Reviews** section, and fill in your rating and comment. The review form is not visible to guests — tap **Sign In** first if you do not see it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My cart is empty after reopening the app — what happened?">
    ShopFront stores your cart locally on your device. If you clear the app's storage, uninstall and reinstall the app, or your device resets that local data, your cart will be empty when you next open the app. To avoid losing your selections, try to complete checkout in the same session. Items in your cart are not reserved, so they may also sell out while sitting in the cart.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update my account information?">
    Open the **Profile** tab and tap the edit option next to the field you want to change. You can update your name, email address, and password from this screen. After making your changes, tap **Save** to apply them. You will need to be signed in to access the Profile tab.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does 'Out of Stock' mean?">
    When a product is marked **Out of Stock**, no units are currently available for purchase. The **Add to Cart** button will be disabled for that item. Check back later — inventory is updated when new stock becomes available, and the button will become active again at that point.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I log out of ShopFront?">
    Open the **Profile** tab and scroll to the bottom of the page. Tap the **Log Out** button to sign out of your account. Logging out clears your session and your cart. You will need to sign in again to access order history or proceed to checkout.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
