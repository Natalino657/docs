---

ShopFront is designed to get you from first launch to a completed order in under five minutes. This guide walks you through downloading the app, creating your account, finding a product, and placing your first order — then shows you where to track it once it is on its way.

<Tip>
  You can browse the full product catalog, read reviews, and add items to your cart without an account. You only need to sign in when you are ready to check out.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download or open the app">
    Get ShopFront on your preferred platform:

    - **iOS**: Search for ShopFront in the Apple App Store and tap **Get**.
    - **Android**: Search for ShopFront in Google Play and tap **Install**.
    - **Web browser**: Navigate to the ShopFront web URL in any modern browser — no installation required.

    Once the app opens, you land on the home screen with the product catalog already visible.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an account">
    Tap **Register** on the welcome screen or from the **Profile** tab if you have already dismissed the welcome flow.

    Fill in the registration form:

    - **Full name** — Your display name for orders and reviews.
    - **Email address** — Used to sign in and receive order confirmations.
    - **Password** — Must be at least eight characters.

    Tap **Create Account**. ShopFront signs you in immediately — no email verification step before you can start shopping.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse and search for products">
    The home screen shows a scrollable product feed. Use the **Search** bar at the top to find something specific — results update as you type.

    To narrow results further, tap a category chip below the search bar. Products load in pages; tap **Load more** at the bottom of any list to continue browsing.

    Tap any product card to open the full product page, where you can see additional images, the full description, pricing, and customer reviews.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to cart and check out">
    From a product page, tap **Add to Cart**. A badge on the cart icon updates to reflect your item count.

    When you are ready to purchase:

    1. Tap the **Cart** tab at the bottom of the screen.
    2. Review your items and adjust quantities if needed, then tap **Checkout**.
    3. On the shipping screen, enter your delivery address and tap **Continue**.
    4. On the payment screen, tap **Pay with PayPal**. ShopFront opens a secure PayPal screen where you authorize the payment.
    5. After PayPal confirms the payment, you are returned to a confirmation screen showing your order number.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track your order">
    After your order is placed, go to the **Profile** tab and tap **My Orders**. Your new order appears at the top of the list with its current status.

    Tap the order to see a full status history. ShopFront updates the status at each stage — from processing through to delivered — so you always know where your package is.
  </Step>
</Steps>
