Tapping a product card on the home screen opens the product detail screen. This page gives you everything you need to evaluate an item before buying — from a large product image to stock status, pricing, and community reviews.

## What the product page shows

The detail screen is organized into three sections that you scroll through from top to bottom.

### Product image

A large image of the item appears at the top of the screen, displayed at full width so you can inspect it clearly.

### Product information

Below the image, a card contains the core details:

- **Name** — the full product title, displayed prominently at the top of the card.
- **Price** — shown in euros alongside the star rating.
- **Star rating** — a row of up to five stars (full, half, or empty) representing the average of all customer ratings, along with the total number of reviews.
- **Description** — a written overview of the product covering its features and specifications.
- **Stock status** — clearly marked as **in stock** or **out of stock**. When the item is in stock, a quantity selector also appears so you can choose how many units to add.

### In stock vs. out of stock

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="In stock" icon="circle-check">
    The quantity selector is visible. Choose your desired quantity and tap **Add to Cart** to continue.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Out of stock" icon="circle-x">
    The **Add to Cart** button is disabled and grayed out. You cannot add the item until it is restocked.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Adding the item to your cart

When a product is in stock, use the quantity picker to select how many units you want — up to the number currently available — then tap **Add to Cart**. You will be taken directly to your cart so you can review your selections or proceed to checkout.

## Customer reviews and ratings

Below the product information card, you will find all reviews submitted by other customers. Each review shows the reviewer's star rating and their written comment. The star rating displayed on both the product card and this detail page reflects the average across all submitted reviews.

## Writing a review

<Note>
  You must be logged in to your ShopFront account to submit a review.
</Note>

If you are signed in, a button to add a review appears in the reviews section. Tapping it opens a form where you can rate and describe the product.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a star rating">
    Tap a star to set your rating from 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest). A rating is required — you cannot submit without one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write a comment">
    Type your thoughts about the product in the comment field. A comment is required before you can submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your review">
    Tap the submit button. Your review is posted immediately and the ratings on the page update to reflect your contribution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Each account can only submit one review per product. If you have already reviewed an item, the form will close and you will see a notification confirming this.
</Info>
