Your ShopFront cart lets you collect items from across the catalog, adjust quantities, and review your total before placing an order. You can open your cart at any time by tapping the cart tab in the bottom navigation bar.

<Tip>
  Your cart is saved directly on your device, so your items stay in place even if you close the app and come back later.
</Tip>

## Adding items to your cart

Items are added from the product detail screen. Open any in-stock product, use the quantity picker to select how many units you want, and tap **Add to Cart**. ShopFront takes you straight to the cart screen so you can review what you've added.

## Viewing your cart

Tap the **Cart** tab in the bottom navigation bar to open your cart at any time. Each item appears as a row showing:

- A thumbnail image of the product
- The product name (shortened if it is long)
- The unit price in euros
- A quantity picker
- A trash icon to remove the item

If your cart is empty, a message lets you know and offers a link to go back to browsing.

## Updating item quantity

Each cart item has a quantity picker on the right side of its row. Tap the picker and select a new value to update the quantity. The options are limited to the number of units currently in stock for that product. The order summary at the bottom updates immediately when you make a change.

## Removing an item

Tap the trash icon on the right side of any cart row to remove that item entirely. The item is deleted right away and the order summary recalculates automatically.

<Warning>
  Removing an item cannot be undone from within the cart. If you remove something by mistake, go back to the product page and add it again.
</Warning>

## Order summary

At the bottom of the cart screen, the **Order Summary** section shows:

- **Items** — the total number of individual units across all products in your cart.
- **Total** — the combined price of everything, calculated from each item's unit price multiplied by its quantity.

The summary updates in real time as you change quantities or remove items.

## Proceeding to checkout

When you are ready to buy, tap **Proceed to Checkout** at the bottom of the order summary.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Make sure your cart is not empty">
    The checkout button is disabled when your cart is empty. Add at least one item before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in if prompted">
    If you are not currently logged in, tapping **Proceed to Checkout** will take you to the sign-in screen. After you log in, you will be sent directly to the shipping step without losing your cart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the checkout flow">
    Once signed in, you will move through the shipping and payment steps to complete your order.
  </Step>
</Steps>
