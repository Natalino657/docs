The home screen is your starting point for discovering everything available in ShopFront. When you open the app, you'll see the full product catalog displayed as a scrollable two-column grid, each card giving you a quick snapshot of the item before you tap into it.

## Searching for products

Use the search bar in the header at the top of the home screen to find specific products by keyword. As you type and submit your search, the grid updates to show only matching results. To clear a search and return to the full catalog, delete your search text and search again with an empty term.

## Navigating pages

When the catalog spans multiple pages, a row of numbered page buttons appears at the bottom of the grid, below the last product row. The current page is highlighted. Tap any page number to jump directly to that page. Pagination controls only appear when there is more than one page of results — searches with a small number of matches will show everything on a single page without any controls.

## What each product card shows

Every card in the grid displays the following at a glance:

## Tapping a product

Tap anywhere on a product card to open the full product detail screen, where you can read the description, check stock, browse reviews, and add the item to your cart.

## When no results are found

If your search returns no matches — or if the catalog is temporarily unavailable — an informational message appears in place of the product grid. Try adjusting your search terms or clearing the search to browse the full catalog again.

<Info>
  The product list refreshes automatically whenever you change your search keyword or navigate to a different page, so you always see up-to-date availability and pricing.
</Info>
