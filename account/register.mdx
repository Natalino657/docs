Creating a ShopFront account lets you place orders and track them from your device. Registration takes only a moment and signs you in automatically as soon as it's complete.

<Tip>
  You can browse and explore products without an account. You only need to register when you're ready to check out.
</Tip>

## How to register

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Register screen">
    From the Sign In screen, tap the **Register** link at the bottom. You can also reach the Register screen directly from the checkout flow if you are not yet signed in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your name">
    Type your full name into the **Name** field. This is the name associated with your account and shown on your profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type a valid email address into the **Email Address** field. Your email is used to sign in and cannot be shared with another account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a password">
    Enter a password in the **Password** field. Tap the eye icon on the right side of the field to show or hide what you're typing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your password">
    Re-enter the same password in the **Confirm Password** field. The app checks that both entries match before submitting. If they don't match, you will see an error and will need to correct them before continuing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Register">
    Tap the **Register** button. If all fields are valid and the email address is not already in use, your account is created and you are signed in immediately. You will be taken to the screen you were on before starting registration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Already have an account?

If you already registered, tap the **Login** link at the bottom of the Register screen to go to the Sign In screen instead.
