The Profile tab is your hub for managing your account. From here you can update your personal details, navigate to your order history, and sign out of the app.

## Accessing your profile

Tap the **Profile** tab in the bottom navigation bar. If you are not signed in, you will be prompted to log in before your profile information is shown.

## Updating your account information

To change your name, email, or password, tap **Account Information** from the Profile menu.

On the Account Information screen you can edit the following fields:

- **Name** — the display name associated with your account
- **Email** — the email address used to sign in
- **Password** — leave this blank if you do not want to change your current password; enter a new password and confirm it to update it

When you are done making changes, tap the **Update** button. A confirmation message appears at the top of the screen if the update is successful. If there is an error, a message will describe what went wrong.

<Warning>
  If you think someone else may have access to your account, update your password immediately from the Account Information screen.
</Warning>

## Logging out

To sign out, tap **Logout** at the bottom of the Profile menu. You will be returned to the home screen and your session will end. Your cart and saved data are cleared when you log out.
