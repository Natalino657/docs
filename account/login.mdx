Signing in to ShopFront gives you access to your cart, lets you place orders, and shows your full order history. You need an active account to check out.

## How to sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Sign In screen">
    The Sign In screen appears automatically when you try to check out without being signed in. You can also reach it from the **Profile** tab if you are not currently logged in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type the email address you used when you registered into the **Email Address** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your password">
    Type your password into the **Password** field.

    <Note>
      Tap the eye icon on the right side of the password field to toggle between showing and hiding your password. This makes it easier to verify what you've typed before submitting.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Sign In">
    Tap the **Sign In** button. If your credentials are correct, you will be signed in and taken back to the screen you were viewing before, or to the home screen if you opened the Sign In screen directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If sign-in fails

If you enter incorrect credentials, an error message appears at the top of the screen. Check that your email address is spelled correctly and that you are using the right password, then try again.

## Don't have an account yet?

If you haven't registered yet, tap the **Register** link at the bottom of the Sign In screen to create a new account.
