## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Register with your email and a password to unlock checkout and order history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and add to cart">
    Search for products by keyword, read reviews, and add items to your cart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check out with PayPal">
    Enter a shipping address, confirm payment via PayPal, and place your order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your order">
    View real-time order status and delivery updates from your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ShopFront is available on iOS, Android, and web. No additional downloads are required to shop on the web.
</Note>
