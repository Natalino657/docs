---
title: "Choose a Payment Method on ShopFront"
description: "Step two of ShopFront checkout: select PayPal as your payment method. No card details are stored by ShopFront — everything is handled by PayPal securely."
sidebarTitle: "Payment"
---

After entering your shipping address, ShopFront takes you to the Payment screen to select how you want to pay. This is the second step of checkout. Once you confirm your payment method, you'll move to the order review screen where you can verify everything before placing your order.

<Note>
  ShopFront never stores your payment details. All payment information is entered directly through PayPal's secure interface, and ShopFront has no access to your card or account credentials at any point.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the payment screen">
    The Payment screen opens automatically after you tap **Continue** on the Shipping screen. If you try to access this screen without a saved shipping address, ShopFront will redirect you back to the Shipping screen first.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select PayPal">
    PayPal is the available payment method. It appears pre-selected in the list. Tap the **PayPal or Credit Card** option to confirm your choice — the radio button will fill to indicate it is selected.

    <Info>
      PayPal lets you pay using your PayPal balance, a linked bank account, or a credit or debit card. You choose which funding source to use when the PayPal payment screen opens in a later step.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Continue">
    Tap **Continue** to save your payment method selection and proceed to the order review screen, where you can see your full order summary before confirming.
  </Step>
</Steps>
