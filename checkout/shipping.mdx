---
title: "Enter Your Shipping Address at Checkout"
description: "Step one of ShopFront checkout: enter your delivery address including street, city, postal code, and country before selecting a payment method."
sidebarTitle: "Shipping"
---

Before ShopFront can process your order, it needs to know where to send your items. This is the first step of the checkout flow, reached by tapping **Proceed to Checkout** from your cart. You'll fill in four address fields and then move on to selecting a payment method.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the shipping screen">
    From your cart, tap **Proceed to Checkout**. The Shipping screen opens and displays any address you may have saved from a previous session. If this is your first order, all fields will be blank.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your street address">
    Tap the **Address** field and type your full street address, including any apartment or unit number. This is the first line of your delivery address.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your city">
    Tap the **City** field and type the name of your city or town.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your postal code">
    Tap the **Postal code** field and type your postal or ZIP code. The keyboard switches to numeric entry automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your country">
    Tap the **Country** field and type your country name.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Continue">
    Once all four fields are filled in, tap **Continue** to save your address and move to the payment selection screen.

    <Note>
      All four fields are required. If you leave any field blank and tap **Continue**, ShopFront will display an error notification and keep you on the Shipping screen until every field is completed.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Your shipping address is saved for the current session. If you navigate back from a later checkout step, your address will still be pre-filled when you return to this screen.
</Tip>
